The Pitch
Our project, StudyLab, is a unified academic companion platform built for students who struggle with fragmented study materials and for mentors who lack visibility into daily student engagement. The system manages data across two clients: an Android app for mentees to track subjects, vault files, and habit scores, and a web dashboard for mentors to manage assignments, tasks, and activity logs. Unlike a basic CRUD application, our design ensures robust data integrity and actively handles complex operational relationships. We implemented a fully normalized multi-tenant schema using bridge tables to manage many-to-many relationships, including a self-referential link enforcing strict mentor-mentee assignments. To support high-volume operations securely, we utilize ACID-compliant transactions with specific commit and rollback logic for multi-table writes, and leverage user-defined scalar functions and indexing to calculate real-time analytics—like consecutive-day study streaks—without creating data redundancy.
